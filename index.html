Get Started

Streamline’s innovative approach to the submittal process is built on the fact that you cannot begin any project without knowing what you need on site and when those items are required on your jobsite.

The first thing Streamline does is import your project schedule directly into the system and tie every activity in that schedule to a material. This ensures that no item is holding up your project due to approvals by the design team. All documents are uploaded, saved, tracked, and statuses directly in Streamline so you don’t spend another minute chasing down files and attachments in emails.

Whole Product Tracking. It’s New.

A new concept developed exclusively by Streamline, “Component Based Building,” means setting up the submittal log so that each submittal type can be broken down into lists and “components” to identify each specific material being used rather than lumping all “like” submittals into a package. With the “submittal package” approach, a few “incorrect” or “undecided” materials hold up an entire package! Remember that 10 week light fixture from Span that you need released last week that is being held up so the Owner can pick the finish of the bathroom light in the CEO office? Not anymore.

Learn more.
